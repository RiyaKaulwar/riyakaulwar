projects

visionxcelerate glasses

AI-powered smart glasses designed to enhance independence and safety for elderly dementia patients while easing the burden on caregivers. integrating fall detection, GPS geo-fencing, and real-time caregiver alerts with an AI assistant that uses voice cloning to mimic loved ones’ voices, alongside face, object, and voice recognition to help patients navigate daily life.
